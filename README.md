# Privacy Policy for Weekly Gacha

**Last Updated: December 10, 2024**

## Introduction

Weekly Gacha ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Weekly Gacha.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information Automatically Collected

**Google Account Information (via Google Sign-In):**
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile picture URL
- Google User ID

**Device Information:**
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifiers

**Usage Data:**
- App usage statistics
- Feature interaction data
- Performance metrics
- Crash reports

**Advertising Information (via Google AdMob):**
- Advertising ID
- Ad interaction data
- Device information for advertising purposes

### 2. Information You Provide

**Game Progress Data:**
- Collected cards and collection status
- Gacha pull history
- Used coupon codes
- In-app settings and preferences

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery**: Provide and maintain app functionality
- **Account Management**: Authenticate users and manage accounts
- **Cloud Synchronization**: Sync data across multiple devices
- **Personalization**: Customize user experience
- **Analytics**: Analyze usage patterns to improve the app
- **Advertising**: Display personalized ads via Google AdMob
- **Customer Support**: Respond to inquiries and provide technical support
- **Legal Compliance**: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

### Service Providers

- Purpose: Authentication, database, cloud storage
- Privacy Policy: [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

**Google AdMob (Google LLC)**
- Purpose: In-app advertising
- Privacy Policy: [AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data in transit is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- **Firebase Security Rules**: Database access controls
- **Secure Authentication**: OAuth 2.0 authentication via Google

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained until you delete your account
- **Game Progress Data**: Deleted when you delete your account
- **Usage Statistics**: Anonymized and retained for up to 2 years
